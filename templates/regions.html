Regions of Kyrgyzstan

We focus on the most beautiful and accessible regions of Kyrgyzstan — perfect for first-time visitors and independent travelers.

Issyk-Kul Region

Alpine lake, sandy beaches, red canyons and easy 1–2 day hikes — the best starting point for exploring Kyrgyzstan.

  • Skazka Canyon (Fairy Tale)

  • Beach villages & viewpoints

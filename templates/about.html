About Kyrgyz Horizont

Independent trip planning service created by a local traveler — built with passion, honesty and deep love for Kyrgyzstan.

Meet the Founder — Erbol

I was born and raised in Kyrgyzstan, spending most of my childhood in the mountains. Over the years, I explored Issyk-Kul, Ala-Kul, Song-Kol, Skazka Canyon and many remote valleys — and now I help travelers experience them safely and meaningfully.

  • Local traveler with real on-the-ground experience
  • Helps build safe, honest and realistic itineraries
  • Understands local culture, drivers, prices and hidden places
  • Supports travelers on WhatsApp throughout the trip

My goal is simple: to make travel in Kyrgyzstan easier, safer and more meaningful — without tourist traps or overpriced agencies.
